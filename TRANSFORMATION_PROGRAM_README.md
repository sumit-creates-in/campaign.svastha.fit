# 12 Week Total Body Transformation Program Landing Page

## Overview
High-converting landing page for the 12 Week Total Body Transformation Program by Sumit Sharma, founder of Strong By Yoga. Targeted at participants who completed the 21 Day Weight Loss Challenge.

## Route
`/12-week-Total-Body-Transformation-Program`

Full URL: `https://campaign.svastha.fit/12-week-Total-Body-Transformation-Program/`

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- Headline: "Your Real Transformation Begins Now"
- Subheadline targeting 21-day challenge participants
- 4 key benefits with checkmarks
- Primary CTA button
- Trust line with social proof

### 2. Big Realization Section
- Explains why people regain weight after short challenges
- Highlights the difference between 21 days vs 90 days
- Positions the 12-week program as the solution

### 3. Achievements Section
- 6 key outcomes participants can achieve
- Icon-based grid layout
- Results: 5-12 kg weight loss

### 4. Program Structure Section
- 3-phase timeline visualization
- Week 1-4: Foundation Phase
- Week 5-8: Fat Burning Phase
- Week 9-12: Transformation Phase

### 5. Program Options Section (Pricing)
- **Group Transformation Plan**: Structured guidance with community support
- **VIP Transformation Plan**: Personal dietitian + 1-on-1 support (Most Popular)
- Clear feature comparison
- Expected results for each plan

### 6. Coach Section
- Introduction to Sumit Sharma
- Credentials and expertise
- Approach blending yoga, science, and lifestyle coaching

### 7. Success Stories Section
- 3 testimonials with before/after results
- Star ratings and quotes
- Social proof banner

### 8. Bonuses Section
- Bonus 1: Fat Loss Recipe Guide
- Bonus 2: Home Yoga Practice Library
- Bonus 3: Mindful Eating Masterclass
- Exclusive for challenge participants

### 9. Urgency Section
- Limited-time offer messaging
- Enrollment closing soon
- Limited spots available

### 10. Final CTA Section
- Dual CTA buttons (Group + VIP)
- Motivational messaging
- Trust indicators

### 11. Sticky CTA (Mobile)
- Appears after scrolling 500px
- Quick access to pricing section

## Design Features

- **Color Scheme**: Emerald green primary, white background, warm accent colors
- **Typography**: Bold headlines, clean readable body text
- **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive design
- **Animations**: Fade-in effects, hover states, smooth transitions
- **CTAs**: Multiple strategically placed call-to-action buttons
- **Trust Elements**: Social proof, testimonials, coach credentials
